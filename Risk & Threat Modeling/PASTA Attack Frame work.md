**PASTA** stands for **P**rocess for **A**ttack **S**imulation and **T**hreat **A**nalysis (PASTA). It is a risk-centric threat modeling method, meaning that risk plays a central role and the focus is on the highest and most relevant risks that can affect your business. After all, IT (such as applications, systems, etc.) serve business, and that is their reason for existing.
